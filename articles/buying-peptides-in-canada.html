1. Why domestic shipping matters most

The single biggest practical headache when sourcing in Canada is the border. Orders shipped from overseas can be delayed, held, or seized at customs — which costs you money and time. A Canadian vendor shipping domestically sidesteps that entirely: faster delivery, no border risk, and a real address you can look up. If two vendors look equal, the one shipping inside Canada wins on practicality alone.

2. Testing beats price — every time

A cheap vial of the wrong thing isn't a deal. The peptide market has a documented trust problem: a Certificate of Analysis (COA) only proves a test was done on some sample — not that it came from the vial you'll receive. Before you compare prices, compare testing transparency:

  • Does the COA verify on the lab's own portal, not just a vendor PDF?
  • Is the lab independent (used by many competing vendors), or captive (only this one)?
  • Is there any independent blind / community testing — the gold standard?
