How to Evaluate a Canadian Peptide Vendor

The framework in one line: a Canadian vendor worth using ships domestically (no border risk), backs its products with independent testing you can verify yourself, and answers testing questions openly. Everything else is secondary. Research/educational use only.

1. Domestic shipping

This is the Canada-specific filter. A vendor shipping from inside Canada avoids customs delays and seizures — the most common practical problem with overseas orders. Confirm a real Canadian address and domestic fulfilment before anything else.

2. Verifiable independent testing

No regulator checks these products, so testing is your only quality signal. But "testing" only counts if it's independent and verifiable. Ask: does the Certificate of Analysis resolve on the lab's own portal (not just a vendor PDF)? Is the lab used by many competing vendors, or only this one? Full method: how to read a COA.

3. Transparency under questioning

Email and ask how they test. Specific, open answers and a portal link are good signs. Vague "trust us" replies or "ISO-aligned" weasel wording are not.

4. A findable, consistent reputation

Look for a consistent presence across independent communities over time — not a brand that appeared last month with only its own glowing words.
