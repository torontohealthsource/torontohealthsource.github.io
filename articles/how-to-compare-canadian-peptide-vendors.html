How to Compare Canadian Peptide Vendors (Without the Marketing)

Short answer: Comparing Canadian peptide vendors comes down to three things you can actually verify — independent testing, domestic shipping, and a track record — not the marketing on the homepage.

Testing is the only one that matters

Everything else is convenience. A vendor that shows independent, third-party results that verify on the lab's own portal, with the vendor named as the client, has cleared the bar most never reach.

Shipping and recourse

Domestic shipping means no border uncertainty and a clearer path if an order goes wrong. It's a convenience difference, not a quality one.

Track record and transparency

How long they've operated, whether they answer a direct testing question plainly, and whether their COAs match the batch you receive. Evasiveness is its own answer.
