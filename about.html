About TorontoHealth

TorontoHealth is an independent, reader-focused resource that explains how peptide lab testing actually works — and how to tell a trustworthy vendor from one selling hype — in plain English, for buyers in Canada.

What we cover

How to read a Certificate of Analysis, how to spot a captive or fake testing lab, how COA reuse and theft work, and the practical realities of sourcing peptides domestically in Canada. Everything is educational — we discuss testing transparency and sourcing, never what any compound does to the body.

Our editorial standard

Guides are sourced from public, verifiable information and reviewed before publishing. We describe patterns, never accuse a specific company. We carry affiliate links (clearly disclosed) and earn a commission if you use our code — at no extra cost to you.
