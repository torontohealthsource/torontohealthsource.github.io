# Vendor Communication Red Flags: The Tell Is How They Answer

_The communication red flags that reveal a peptide vendor, and the one question that exposes them. Educational only._

Source: https://torontohealthsource.github.io/articles/peptide-vendor-communication-red-flags.html · Updated: July 8, 2026 · TorontoHealth — educational only, not medical advice.


> **Short answer:** How a vendor answers a simple testing question tells you more than its entire homepage. Evasiveness is the tell.

## Green flags

A direct answer, a link to a COA that verifies on the lab's portal, the vendor named as the client, and a batch number that matches.

## Red flags

Deflection, screenshots instead of verifiable links, in-house-only numbers, or pressure to buy before you can check anything.

## The one question to ask

"Can you link the independent COA for this batch, verifiable on the lab's portal?" A confident vendor answers in one message. The rest reveal themselves.
